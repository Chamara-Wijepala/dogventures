Are the tours safe for dogs?

Absolutely! Our tours are designed with your furry friend’s safety as a top priority. From carefully selected dog-friendly routes to ensuring access to fresh water and shade, we’ve got everything covered. Our guides are well-versed in handling pets, so you can relax and enjoy the adventure, knowing your dog is in safe hands!

Can I bring more than one dog on the trip?

Of course! We understand the joy of traveling with multiple furry companions. Let us know in advance, and we'll make sure to accommodate all your four-legged friends.

What if my dog has specific dietary needs?

No worries at all! We understand that every dog is unique, especially when it comes to diet. Let us know about your dog’s specific dietary needs when booking, and we’ll make the necessary arrangements. Whether it’s special treats, meals, or timing adjustments, we’ll ensure your dog stays healthy and happy throughout the trip.

Are there age restrictions for dogs on the tours?

We believe every dog deserves an adventure, but for their comfort and safety, we do have some age guidelines. Puppies under six months and senior dogs with mobility issues may find certain tours a bit challenging. However, we offer a variety of experiences, from leisurely walks to more active excursions, so you can choose one that suits your dog’s age and energy level perfectly!

Can all dog breeds participate in the tours?

Yes, all breeds are welcome! Our tours are designed to accommodate dogs of all shapes and sizes. That said, some tours might be better suited for specific energy levels or physical capabilities, so we recommend selecting a tour that matches your dog’s temperament and stamina. If you’re unsure, our team is happy to help you choose the perfect adventure for your furry companion!
